Server Rules

Please read and follow the rules to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone.

  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs.
  • Keep chat friendly and free of offensive language.
  • Follow all instructions given by staff members.
  • Use appropriate builds and skins.
  • Report any rule violations to staff.
